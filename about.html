About Me

I’m Amanda Wartick—an interdisciplinary technologist, educator, and systems thinker passionate about building a safer, more inclusive digital future. With over 15 years of experience in leadership roles at Microsoft and AWS, I’ve led high-impact initiatives in marketing strategy, compliance, operations, and program development. Today, I’m focused on cybersecurity and user-centered design through my Information Technology degree at the University of Washington.

My approach blends deep curiosity, ethical intent, and collaborative execution. I believe that technology should be designed not just for efficiency, but with empathy and accessibility at its core. Whether building digital workflows, advocating for secure data systems, or mentoring peers in tech, I care about solving the right problems the right way.

Mission & Values

  • Equity: I center inclusion and access in every project I work on.
  • Accountability: I believe ethical tech requires ownership and transparency.
  • Curiosity: I ask questions to understand root causes and uncover better answers.
  • Impact: I care about results that are sustainable, measurable, and human-centered.

Beyond the Screen

I’m also a former musician, creative strategist, and lifelong learning nerd. When I’m not coding, researching, or mapping workflows, you might find me designing a community workshop, testing cybersecurity tools, or exploring the intersection of AI, ethics, and education.
