Education Assistive Smart Glasses

Prototyping inclusive, voice-driven tech to support focus and learning.

Project Overview

ELA is a conceptual prototype for a wearable device designed to support students with focus, engagement, and accessibility. Built for an academic UX project, the smart glasses blend voice interaction, gesture controls, and inclusive design features inspired by user research and HCI principles. I co-led design, prototyping, and scenario-based testing. This project reflects my interest in ethical, human-centered technology—especially in education and assistive tools.

Role

Lead UX Designer – Accessibility & Interaction

  • Co-led the design and development of a conceptual UX prototype for a wearable assistive device
  • Conducted user research and scenario writing to inform interaction models
  • Designed multimodal features including voice, gesture, and haptic feedback systems
  • Created demo materials, including a product walkthrough video and interactive blueprint
  • Focused on accessibility, inclusivity, and attention-supportive design across all stages
