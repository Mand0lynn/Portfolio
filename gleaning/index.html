Gleaning Project Website Redesign

Improving access, usability, and community connection through a thoughtful website refresh.

Project Overview

Harvest Pierce County is a local nonprofit focused on food access, gleaning, and community resilience. Their website needed a usability overhaul to better serve volunteers and improve form-based workflows. Working with an existing backend, I led the UX and front-end redesign, improving accessibility, form validation, and content clarity. The project reflects a human-centered approach to civic tech and nonprofit support.

Role
Lead Project Manager – Web Modernization

- Oversaw day-to-day progress across the development and PMO teams.
- Facilitated planning, communication, and prioritization throughout the redesign process.
- Helped define goals for usability, accessibility, and continuity with the existing backend system.
- Provided feedback and guidance on front-end structure, layout, and user experience decisions.
- Ensured the project stayed on track and aligned with the client’s needs, timeline, and future vision.
