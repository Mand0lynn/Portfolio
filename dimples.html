Dimples & Dough Website

Bringing a small business to life online with accessible, responsive design.

Project Overview

Dimples & Dough is a small bakery with a growing client base and a need for a digital presence that matched its brand. My team and I worked directly with the owner to design and build a responsive website that highlights products, tells the bakery’s story, and makes it easy for customers to connect. My role spanned layout, accessibility, visual branding, and mobile optimization. The result is a clean, approachable site that sets the stage for future growth.

Role

Lead Technical Program Manager & UX Designer

  • Led the design and development of a responsive, mobile-first website tailored to the client’s vision
  • Built the site using HTML, CSS, and accessibility-first layout techniques
  • Created a visual brand identity through color, typography, and layout choices
  • Worked closely with the client to clarify goals, refine content, and ensure usability
  • Integrated calls to action and navigation patterns that support future business growth
